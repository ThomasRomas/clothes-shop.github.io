Welcome To Khmer Tranditional Clothes Shop

Traditional Khmer clothing is both beautiful and functional. The stypes have been shaped by Cambodia's hot tropical climate,as well as its cultural traditions, Both men and women wear loose.Lightewight clothing made of natural fibers and natural meterial like cotton or silk.Bright colors and intricate datails showcase the skill of khmer weavers and tailors. The most well-known traditional garment is the sampot,a wrap-around lower garment worn by both men and women. The sompot phamoong, worn by women, is a rectangular fabric wrapped pattens or colorful floral bracades.

Sompot

The sampot chang kben is a full, ankle-length skirt.It's made of silk and decoraded with floral or geometric partterns. Women wear this for special occasions like wedding pr festivals. The sampot chang phnom is knee-length cotton skirt.It's more casual and worn for everyday activities.The sampot is wrapped around the waist and secured in place with bell called a kroma.

Sarong

A sarong as large piece of patterned fabric used by Khmer people all genders.It has many uses,such as:A wrap for women to wear over a sleeveless top A towel for bathing A blanket A hammock or mat to sit on.

Ankor Shirt

The Angkor shirt is a loose-fitting silk or cotton button-down shirt.It's worn by both men and women, and often paired with trouser or a sampot.Angkor shirts feature delicate embroidery, especailly around the collar and cuffs. They are propular souvenirs for tourists visiting Cambodia.
